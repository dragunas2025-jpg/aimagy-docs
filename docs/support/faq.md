# Frequently asked questions

## Are the Krita and Photoshop plugins the same?

No. Flash Banana Free and Pro are Krita plugins. AI Generator is a separate
Photoshop plugin.

## Is Flash Banana Free a trial?

Flash Banana Free is a reduced version of Flash Banana Pro with some features
locked. The detailed comparison will be maintained in
[Free vs Pro](/krita/flash-banana/free-vs-pro/).
